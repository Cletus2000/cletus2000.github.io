About Me

My name is Carlos Rocamora Colomer (known online as Cletus2000), and I am a final-year Multimedia Engineering student at the University of Alicante, specializing in video game development.

Ever since I was a child, I’ve been passionate about video games, but over time I realized that what truly fascinated me wasn’t just playing them, but understanding how they were made.

I'm absolutely captivated by the blend of creativity and technology that goes into making video games, so my goal is to create interactive experiences that not only entertain and inspire players, but also makes me learn, discover new ideas, and grow as a professional and as a person.
